Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What We Collect

Decision Tracker collects only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Slack Messages: Only messages that contain decision-related keywords are processed
  • User Information: Slack user IDs and workspace information for proper routing
  • Decision Data: Confirmed decisions, including summary, participants, and context
  • Email Content: When you CC our email address, we process the email content for decisions

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Detect and extract decision information from your messages
  • Store confirmed decisions in your workspace's private database
  • Provide search and retrieval functionality
  • Send confirmation requests and notifications

Data Isolation

Each Slack workspace's data is completely isolated. Teams cannot access each other's decisions or data. All database queries are filtered by workspace ID to ensure complete separation.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Encrypted database connections
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Vercel/PostgreSQL)
  • Access tokens encrypted at rest
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as our app is installed in your workspace. When you uninstall the app, all associated data is automatically marked for deletion within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your workspace's decision data
  • Delete: Request deletion of specific decisions or all data
  • Correct: Request correction of inaccurate decision information
  • Export: Request your data in a portable format

Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For decision detection and extraction (data is not stored by OpenAI)
  • SendGrid: For sending confirmation emails
  • Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure
  • PostgreSQL: For secure data storage

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:

Email: privacy@set4.io
Response Time: Within 72 hours

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or email.
